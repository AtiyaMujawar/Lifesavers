About Website

Personality

At its most basic, personality is the characteristic patterns of thoughts, feelings, and behaviors that make a person unique. It is believed that personality arises from within the individual and remains fairly consistent throughout life.

Personality Test

Personality tests provide measures of such characteristics as feelings and emotional states, preoccupations, motivations, attitudes, and approaches to interpersonal relations.Often used to gain insight into who people are and what motivates them.

"This website provides a collection of interactive personality tests with detailed results that can be taken for personal entertainment or to learn more about personality assessment. A special focus is given to the strengths, weaknesses and validity of the various systems."
